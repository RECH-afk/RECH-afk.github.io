About me

Hi! I'm RECH, an indie developer who makes games that make even the most sane game designers' eye twitch.

I had nothing to do when I was 9 years old, and being such a genius, I made a... how can I put it more gently... "game" on my phone. If, of course, you call a slideshow of pixelated poop that was supposed to scare you a game.

Now I would erase that "masterpiece" and pretend it never existed. But no, it's somewhere out there... and maybe it still scares someone. But not as a game, but as proof that children shouldn't be given technology.

Anyway, in short: I make games because my brain is designed in such a way that it can't NOT create this crap. I would stop if I could. But I can't. And I don't want to)
